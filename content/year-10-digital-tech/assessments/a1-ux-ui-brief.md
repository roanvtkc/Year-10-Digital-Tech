# Minor A1: UX/UI Brief and Prototype (Student)

## What you will do
You will identify a real client need and design a user experience (UX) and interface (UI) that responds to it. This task feeds into your major project proposal.

## Deliverables
- Client needs summary (evidence from interviews, surveys, or observations)
- Design brief with constraints and success criteria
- UX flow and wireframes
- UI prototype (low or mid fidelity)
- Short evaluation against your criteria

## Success criteria
- Evidence clearly supports the client need
- UX/UI design aligns to the needs and constraints
- Prototype is clear and testable
- Evaluation uses criteria and feedback

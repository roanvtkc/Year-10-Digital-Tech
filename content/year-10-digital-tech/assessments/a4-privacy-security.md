# Minor A4: Privacy and Security Case Study (Student)

## What you will do
You will analyse a digital system using the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and identify security risks, including supply chain vulnerabilities.

## Deliverables
- Case study selection and system summary
- APP-based critique of data collection and use
- Risk analysis including supply chain vulnerabilities
- Recommendations for improvement

## Success criteria
- APPs are applied accurately
- Risks are clearly linked to system design
- Recommendations are practical and justified

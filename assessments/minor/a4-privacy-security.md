# Minor A4: Privacy and Security Case Study

## Purpose
Critique a digital system using the Australian Privacy Principles and evaluate security risks including supply chain vulnerabilities.

## Outcome tags
PS10-1, PS10-2, DS10-1

## Deliverables
- Case study selection and system summary
- APP-based critique of data collection and use
- Risk analysis including supply chain vulnerabilities
- Recommendations for improvement

## Evidence of learning
- Correct interpretation of APPs
- Clear link between risks and system design
- Actionable recommendations

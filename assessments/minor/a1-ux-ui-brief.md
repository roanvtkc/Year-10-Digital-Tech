# Minor A1: UX/UI Brief and Prototype

## Purpose
Define a real client need and design a user experience and interface that addresses it. This task feeds directly into the major project proposal.

## Outcome tags
ID10-1 (WA10DIGDTID1), ID10-2 (WA10DIGDTID2), ID10-3 (WA10DIGDTID3), DI10-1 (WA10DIGDI1), DI10-2 (WA10DIGDI2), DSN10-1 (WA10DIGDTDE1), EV10-1 (WA10DIGDTEV1), PM10-1 (WA10DIGDTPM1)

## Deliverables
- Client needs summary (interviews, surveys, or other data gathering)
- Design brief with constraints and success criteria
- UX flow and wireframes
- UI prototype (low or mid fidelity)
- Reflection and evaluation against criteria

## Evidence of learning
- Rationale for design choices
- Feedback cycles and iteration notes
- Clear mapping to client needs
